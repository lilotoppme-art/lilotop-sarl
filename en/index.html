Official profile

Building reliable bridges between international standards and Congolese field realities.

LILOTOP SARL is a Congolese-owned company supporting mining companies, contractors, institutions, banks, SMEs and international partners with goods supply, reliable supplier search, logistics coordination and commercial facilitation.

To become a leading Congolese platform for mining supply, logistics services, technical partnerships and business digitalization in Central Africa.

Working method

Clear governance from needs analysis to after-delivery support.

Each file is handled through a risk, compliance and execution lens.

  1. Needs analysisTechnical specifications, timelines, logistics constraints and documentation requirements.
  2. Solution searchIdentification of suitable suppliers, partners or service providers.
  3. Comparison and selectionEvaluation of price, quality, lead time, certificates, incoterm and risk level.
  4. Commercial proposalClear offer with conditions, timing, available documents and responsibilities.
  5. Operational follow-upOrder, shipment, transit, delivery or service execution.
  6. After-delivery supportQuality follow-up, claims, documentation and additional needs.
